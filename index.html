Through extensive research, we identified three major challenges that are holding back the recruiting industry and have been developing a mobile-first recruiting platform to address them.

Finally, after decades of the same old approach, Vanda is bringing the recruiting industry into the 21st century for both job seekers and recruiters through its new-generation, community based recruiting platform. This new platform delivers innovative security and incentive structures built on the Ethereum blockchain and creates a paradigm shift in the way recruiting will be done in the future.

Finding Candidates

Keyword searches deliver

benefits

Vanda combines smart, contextual search, blockchain-powered incentives and security infrastructure to deliver a better experience for recruiters and job seekers

Recruiters

Vanda allows recruiters to search a large pool of active and inactive job seekers, find qualified candidates based on smarter and more efficient search and build valuable, longterm relationships with candidates over time.

Employees

Most employed professionals let their job site profiles expire and become outdated as soon as they find a job, making it impossible for recruiters to find them through traditional methods. No wonder employee referrals account for almost half of all hires while only being 7% of the incoming applications! Vanda creates financial incentives for professionals who are already employed to keep their profile current and even improve hireability through professional development opportunities. This creates shared value between employees and recruiters, powered by Vanda’s incentive and community-based approach to modern recruiting.

Job Seekers

For job seekers who are actively searching for a new job, the benefits of Vanda are quite clear. The entire platform exists as a mobile experience and allows candidates to reach more recruiters without sifting through countless irrelevant job listings and filling out lengthy online applications that will never be seen by an actual human being!

value The Vanda
Value Proposition

Innovative

Vanda’s unique approach to recruiting will fill a massive gap in the market, increasing the efficiency of web-based recruiting to that of employee referrals and internal hires.

Mobile-First

Vanda is a mobile-first community-based platform, providing an intuitive experience for candidates and recruiters that goes beyond mobile job listings.

Secure

Vanda leverages the power of a proprietary blockchain to ensure all sensitive and private data is secured on both the recruiter and job seeker side.

Smart

Vanda’s contextual search algorythm allows job seekers to find jobs based on their specific skillset, experience and interest, while giving recruiters the necessary tools to avoid sifting through unqualified candidate leads.

Incentive Structure

The platform creates a new approach for recruiters and candidates to connect, using token incentives to encourage referrals from users and identify candidates who aren’t actively looking.

Scalable

The Vanda platform will change the way recruiting is done around the world. Our platform will be translated into multiple languages, and will be able to scale quickly as we grow our user base.

Product

Candidate profiles will be deeper and more complete than ever, driven by token incentives and reward structures.

Recruiters will have a shorter list of highly qualified candidates to choose from, and only contact candidates directly when they’re serious about considering them for the job. No more wasted time.

Recruiters are connected with candidates once they request direct access and pay using the Vanda tokens, opening up an instant and direct communication channel to begin the interview process.
